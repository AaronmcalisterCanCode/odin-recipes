Dough

Description

Dough is a thick, malleable mixture. It's used as a base for many baked goods such as bread, pizza, pastries, and cookies. Depending on its purpose, dough can be soft and sticky or firm and elastic.

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Activate the yeast
  2. Mix the dough
  3. Knead
  4. First rise
  5. Use or shape
  6. Bake
