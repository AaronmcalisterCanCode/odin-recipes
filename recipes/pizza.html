Description

This meat pizza is loaded with pepperoni, sausage, and ground beef.

Ingredients

  • Pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pizza crust
  • 1 to 1 1/2 cups of shredded motzarela
  • 1/2 cup cooked ground beef
  • cup pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup cooked and crumbled italian sausage

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Preheat Oven
  3. Shape the dough
  4. Assemble the pizza
  5. Bake
  6. Serve
